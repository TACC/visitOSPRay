What is visitOSPRay?

visitOSPRay is a module for the VisIt visualization toolkit (http://visit.llnl.gov/) that utilizes Intel's OSPRay ray tracing framework (http://ospray.github.io) for rendering. This offers a performant CPU rendering package with enhanced image quality.

visitOSPRay is developed by the Texas Advanced Computing Center at the University of Texas at Austin, in partnership with Intel Corporation.
